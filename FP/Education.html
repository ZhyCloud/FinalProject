Introduction

Guangdong University of Technology (English: Guangdong University of Technology , abbreviation : GDUT ), abbreviated as Guanggong University , Guangzhou University of Technology , is a public engineering research university in mainland China , located in Guangzhou and Jieyang, Guangdong Province , the People's Republic of China , affiliated to Guangdong Province Department of Education . [3] Guangdong University of Technology was founded in 1958. It has now developed into a university with engineering as its main subject and a multi-disciplinary and coordinated development of engineering, economics, management, literature, law and art. It is a “211 Project” university in Guangdong Province, and there are currently 13 high- level universities in Guangdong Province. one.
The history of Guangdong University of Technology can be traced back to Guangzhou Higher Technical School in 1942. Its predecessor, Guangdong Institute of Technology, was founded in 1958 on the basis of Guangzhou Higher Industrial School. The first party secretary and dean of the school was Zeng Zhi ; in 1962, the school developed into Guangdong Institute of Technology, with Mai Yunyu as the head. In 1970, due to the influence of the Cultural Revolution , the school moved to Qujiang , Shaoguan , and was renamed Guangdong Institute of Mining and Metallurgy; in 1982, the school moved back to Guangzhou, renamed Guangdong Institute of Technology, and resumed the original site; in 1995, Guangdong Institute of Technology and Guangdong Institute of Mechanical Engineering , the East Institute of South China Construction Institute was established as Guangdong University of Technology.

School of Computer Science

The School of Computer Science of Guangdong University of Technology is a secondary college under Guangdong University of Technology and a member of the Guangdong Province Supercomputing Application Technology Innovation Alliance.

In 1984, the school established the computer application major. In 1990, the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of Guangdong Institute of Technology was established. In 2000, the School of Computer Science of Guangdong University of Technology was established.

According to the official website of the college in August 2021, the college has 4 teaching departments and 5 undergraduate majors; there are 1 second-level discipline doctoral program, 2 first-level discipline master programs, 2 engineering master degree fields, and 2 professional degrees. There are 133 faculty members, including 23 professors, 34 associate professors, 8 doctoral supervisors, 63 master supervisors, and 82 teachers with doctoral degrees; there are 3972 students, including 3250 undergraduates , 722 graduate students.
